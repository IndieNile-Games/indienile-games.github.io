Meet TheLettuceMan!

What's good? I'm Gavin Smith, more widely known as Lettuce. I kinda do the nerd stuff around these parts.

IndieNile Games was originally known as LettuceMan Studios (picked because I use to be the only one working on it.) We recently updated our online presence a bit, and got a long-overdue redesign, and in turn dropped the old name for the new one!

I started learning computer engineering in 5th grade and it just stuck with me. After a couple years of work, my skills were good enough to start game development. My first few were... questionable at best... but with the help of our small team we can make some pretty fun things!

You can find me on Twitter and on My Website.
